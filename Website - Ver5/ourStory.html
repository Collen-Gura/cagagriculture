CAG AGRIBUSINESS

CAG Farms (Pvt) Limited, trading as CAG Agribusiness, is a subsidiary of CAG Holdings (Pvt) limited.

CAG Holdings was incorporated in Zimbabwe in 2002.

The holding company, CAG Holdings, or The Group, has business interests in agribuness, engineering, signage and properties.

CAG Farms is the Group's investment vehicle in everything agriculture. On cereals, CAG Agriculture grows wheat, maize and soya bean, while on vegetables it grows tomatoes, cabbages, potatoes, mangetout peas, runner beans and sugar snap. In addition to row crops, the company grows oranges, mangoes and macadamia nuts.

CAG Agribusiness has established an Agricultural magazine to be published monthly. The main objective of the magazine, CAG AGRICULTURE, is the provision free information to farmers to assist with informed short- and long-term decision-making.
